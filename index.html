06 / FAQ

Questions you should ask.

Is it really free to search?

Yes. Full tender search, all provinces, no signup and no card. We only charge for the intelligence layer: the AI agent, per-tender eligibility checks and the compliance vault. We will never gate basic discovery.

Where does the tender data come from?

Directly from the National Treasury eTenders portal via its official open data API (OCDS standard), plus provincial portals and metro municipality sites. Synced throughout the day.

How is this different from eTenders.gov.za?

The government portal lists tenders. Vula matches them to your business, checks whether you qualify against each one's B-BBEE, CSD and CIDB requirements, reads the tender pack for you, and tells you what to fix when you fall short. The portal is a noticeboard. Vula is a bid team.

Do I need to be CSD registered to use Vula?

No. You can search and get matched without it. When you are ready to bid, CSD registration is required by government, and we can handle it for you as a once-off service (R800).

Will this actually help me win tenders?

Most SMMEs lose bids on compliance, not price: a missing returnable document, an expired certificate, a B-BBEE level that concedes preference points. Vula attacks exactly that. We show you which tenders fit you, whether you qualify, and the precise gap when you don't.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no notice periods, no phone calls. Cancel from your dashboard in two clicks and keep access until the end of your billing month.
